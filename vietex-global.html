Who we are

We make sourcing from Vietnam simple, safe and reliable.

Vietex Global is a Hanoi-based agricultural and seafood export company. We are not a farm and not a factory — we are the dedicated partner that sits between carefully selected Vietnamese producers and buyers around the world.

Sourcing directly overseas creates real friction: language barriers, inconsistent quality, unfamiliar paperwork and the risk of a shipment failing inspection on arrival. Vietex removes that friction. We vet suppliers, control quality before loading, prepare complete export documentation, and stay accountable for the consignment until it reaches you.

Our existing customers in Poland (EU) and the United Arab Emirates (GCC) are proof that we already meet two of the world's most demanding sets of import standards.

Export & logistics

Documented, ready to clear customs.

Vietex manages the full export process so your consignment arrives correctly documented. Here is what we handle for every shipment.

FOB, CFR, CIF and DAP — quoted to suit your preference
Departure ports
Hai Phong (North) and Cat Lai / Ho Chi Minh City (South)
Container options
20′ and 40′, including reefer for chilled / frozen product; LCL on request
Payment terms
Commonly T/T or irrevocable L/C; trial-order terms available
Documentation we prepare
Commercial invoice Packing list Bill of lading Certificate of origin EUR.1 / AANZFTA Phytosanitary Fumigation Cert. of analysis Halal (where applicable)

We prepare consignments with Australia's biosecurity (DAFF / BICON) import conditions in mind — including correct treatment, fumigation and phytosanitary documentation — to support smooth quarantine clearance.
